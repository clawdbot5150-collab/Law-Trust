Editorial Policy

How we review legal document services — our methodology, standards, and commitment to independence

Our Independence Promise

Law-Trust.com is an independent editorial team, not a law firm. We exist to help families make informed decisions about online legal document services.

We never accept paid placements. Our rankings are determined solely by our testing process and expert analysis. While we earn affiliate commissions when you click our links, this never influences our editorial decisions, rankings, or scores.

How We Review Services

Every service we review goes through the same rigorous testing process:

1. Hands-On Testing

We create actual legal documents using every service we review. Our team completes the full process from account creation through document download, evaluating:

  • Ease of account setup
  • Clarity of questionnaire (legal jargon vs. plain language)
  • Time required to complete documents
  • Quality of help resources and tooltips
  • Document generation speed and format options

2. Legal Validity Review

Our legal team reviews every generated document to verify:

  • Compliance with jurisdiction-specific requirements
  • Presence of required clauses and witness provisions
  • Accuracy of legal language and definitions
  • Proper handling of complex scenarios (blended families, international assets, digital assets)

3. Customer Support Evaluation

We test every support channel offered:

  • Live chat response time and quality
  • Email support turnaround (we send real questions)
  • Phone support availability and helpfulness
  • Quality of help center documentation

4. Price Transparency Analysis

We evaluate pricing for:

  • Clarity of pricing structure (no hidden fees)
  • Value for money compared to alternatives
  • Refund policy fairness
  • Upsell pressure during checkout

5. Jurisdictional Coverage Check

For international services, we verify:

  • Which countries and states/provinces are supported
  • Accuracy of jurisdiction-specific clauses
  • Proper handling of cross-border estates

Our Scoring Rubric

Each service receives a score out of 100 based on weighted criteria:

Criteria Weight What We Evaluate
Legal Validity 30% Document compliance with jurisdiction requirements, legal accuracy, proper clauses
Ease of Use 25% Interface clarity, questionnaire simplicity, time required, help resources
Price & Value 20% Pricing transparency, value vs. alternatives, refund policy, no hidden fees
Customer Support 15% Response time, helpfulness, channel availability, documentation quality
Document Quality 10% Formatting, customization options, PDF quality, storage/access features

Update Frequency

We update every review at least every 6 months. For high-traffic reviews or when a service makes significant changes (pricing, features, legal validity), we update immediately.

Every review displays the last updated date at the top of the article.

Affiliate Disclosure

Law-Trust.com earns affiliate commissions when you click links to services we review. This is how we fund our research and keep our content free.

Our affiliate relationships do NOT influence our rankings. We apply the same scoring rubric to every service, regardless of commission rates. Some of our lowest-ranked services offer higher commissions than our top picks — we still rank them honestly.

Fact-Checking Standards

All factual claims in our reviews are verified against:

  • Official service websites and documentation
  • Direct testing and screenshots
  • Customer support confirmations
  • Official government sources for legal requirements

If we make an error, we correct it immediately and note the correction at the top of the article.
