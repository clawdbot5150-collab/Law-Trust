Frequently Asked Questions
Are these leads exclusive?

Each lead is delivered to one buyer at a time. We do not sell the same lead to multiple buyers simultaneously. Hot leads (80+) go to the first qualified buyer whose preferences match.

How fast do I receive leads?

Leads are delivered within 60 seconds of form submission via email. You'll receive full contact info, document type, location, budget, and the complete intent score breakdown.

What if a lead is bad quality?

If a lead has an invalid email or clearly fake information, contact us within 24 hours and we'll issue a credit. Our scoring system filters out most low-quality submissions automatically.

How do I add funds?

After registering, you can request a top-up from your dashboard. We'll send you an invoice (card or bank transfer). Leads are deducted from your balance automatically.

Can I filter by document type or state?

Yes — you set your preferences during registration and can update them anytime from your dashboard. Filter by document type, country, state, budget tier, and lead score range.
